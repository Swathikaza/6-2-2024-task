Sakshi.com works as a part and parcel of the media conglomerate which broke new ground in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana by launching the Telugu daily, Sakshi, using state-of-the-art technology, and which revolutionized broadcast journalism by introducing HD television, bringing viewers videos of the highest quality. Sakshi.com is an interactive website which gets you in-depth coverage and follow-up stories from a battery of veteran journalists - on local, national and international events; on moments that make and mar history; on a smart, cool site designed for people on the move and others across all ages and interests, with applications across platforms; on interactive, user-friendly mobile applications. english.sakshi.com is another first on the Sakshi.com website which caters to readers looking for quality content in English, in the Regional, National and International space across news and various other categories of interest. hindi.sakshi.com, the Hindi news portal from the Group, is equally a comprehensive digital national news platform intended to deliver seamless news and information to the Hindi readership across the country. Sakshibusiness.com not only brings you news as it happens but also provides in-depth market analysis and advice on the financial issues that impact your day-to-day life including tax matters and share market recommendations.
